FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to register for every event separately?

Yes — each event has its own registration through the Register page, so make sure you select the correct event from the dropdown.

Is there an entry fee for students?

Most events are free for currently enrolled students with a valid registration number. A few workshops with limited seats may have a nominal fee, noted on the event card.

Can external guests attend?

Select events, such as the cultural gala and the demo day, are open to external guests. Check the event description on the Events page for guest eligibility.

How do I become a sponsor or volunteer?

Choose "Sponsorship" or "Volunteer Opportunities" in the contact form subject dropdown above and our team will follow up within two working days.
